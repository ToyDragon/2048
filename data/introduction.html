Scenario: Introduction

Gridworld is a little environment for you to exercise your python skills.

In this small example we create a "bug" that walks forward and turns 45 degrees.

We do that by taking advantage of the following tools:

  • gridworld.createBug(x, y) - Creates a bug on the grid at the specified position, and returns the bug as an object that you can use to manipulate it later.
  • gridworld.onTick(function) - Calls your specified function repeatedly.
  • bug.setColor(color string) - Renders your bug with the specified color.
  • bug.moveForward() - Advances your bug forward one tile.
  • bug.turnRight() - Turns your bug clockwise 45 degrees.
